# Workflow Checklist

## Step-by-step production flow
1. Identify the exact class, subject, and term.
2. Open the relevant scheme of work document for that class, subject, and term.
3. Extract the main term topics.
4. Draft Section A: 20 objective questions from those topics only.
5. Draft Section B: 5 fill-in-the-gap questions from those topics only.
6. Draft Section C: 5 theory questions, each with (a) and (b).
7. Add the instruction: Answer any THREE (3) questions.
8. Add diagrams directly inside only the questions that need them.
9. Build the paper as a 2-slide deck.
10. Put the full header on slide 1 only.
11. Continue on slide 2 without repeating the header.
12. Review for age level, clarity, spacing, and syllabus alignment.
13. Export the finished slide deck.

## Red-flag checks
- Do not use a diagram bank.
- Do not repeat the header on slide 2.
- Do not add topics not found in the scheme.
- Do not make Section C only 4 questions.
- Do not forget the (a) and (b) subparts.
- Do not make the wording too advanced for the class.

## Final approval checks
- Content matches syllabus
- Difficulty is easy enough
- Diagram placement is correct
- Formatting is consistent
- Output is in slide form
