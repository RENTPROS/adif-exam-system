# Master Template Specification

## Output format
- PowerPoint slide deck
- 2 slides per subject paper
- Slide 1 = page 1
- Slide 2 = page 2 continuation

## Header (slide 1 only)
Use the ADIF logo at the top.

ADIF SCHOOL  
9, Ashafa Egemo Street, Baba Dada Bus Stop, Egan, Lagos  
[FIRST / SECOND / THIRD] TERM EXAMINATION 2025/2026 ACADEMIC SESSION  
Motto: Knowledge is Light

Class: ____________________  
Subject: ____________________  
Session: ______ / ______  
Name: ______________________

## Slide 2 rule
- Do NOT repeat the school header.
- Do NOT redesign page 2 as a fresh paper.
- Slide 2 must continue naturally from slide 1.

## Section structure
### SECTION A — OBJECTIVES
- 20 questions
- Options A, B, C, D
- Keep options neat and readable
- Use flexible spacing if a question contains a diagram

### SECTION B — FILL IN THE GAPS
- 5 questions
- Short, direct answers
- Keep language simple

### SECTION C — THEORY
Instruction line must read:
**Answer any THREE (3) questions.**

Then provide:
1(a) ...  
1(b) ...

2(a) ...  
2(b) ...

3(a) ...  
3(b) ...

4(a) ...  
4(b) ...

5(a) ...  
5(b) ...

## Diagram rule
- No diagram bank
- Every diagram must appear inside the exact question where it is needed
- Add vertical spacing around that question when needed
- Diagrams must be simple, black-and-white, and print-friendly unless the sample style requires otherwise

## Layout preference
- Clean school look
- Bold section headings
- Body text readable for primary school use
- Avoid overcrowding
- Keep the paper to 2 slides/pages total
